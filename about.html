Meet Your Therapist

about tyler boudreau:

Tyler Boudreau is a Licensed Independent Clinical Social Worker practicing in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

He works with individuals, couples, and teens who are navigating anxiety, relationship challenges, life transitions, and the effects of trauma. His approach integrates evidence-based psychotherapy with EMDR, mindfulness, and a deep respect for the mind-body connection.

“

Taking on therapy is a challenging step and the work can certainly be daunting. And…the work can be inspiring as well. It can be fulfilling and empowering and liberating.
